Frequently Asked Questions

What does CleanURL.org do?
We remove tracking parameters like utm_source, fbclid, gclid, and others from URLs to protect your privacy and share clean links.
Is CleanURL.org free to use?
Yes! CleanURL.org is 100% free, with no login required.
Do you store any of my data?
No, we never store, log, or track any URLs you paste or clean. Your privacy is our top priority.
Can I use CleanURL.org on mobile?
Absolutely. The tool is fully responsive and works smoothly on all mobile devices.
What are tracking parameters?
Tracking parameters like utm_source or fbclid are added to URLs by marketers to track clicks. CleanURL.org removes these to give you a simpler, private link.
